Basic Survival Skills

Course Code: ERU-SUR-04

Course Introduction

This module is designed to ensure ERU responders can sustain themselves and their teams in austere environments. Training focuses on wilderness survival, land navigation without digital aids, and managing resources during prolonged disaster deployments.

Core Competencies

  • • Map Reading & Compass Navigation
  • • Shelter Construction
  • • Water Procurement & Purification
  • • Field Sanitation & Hygiene

Environment Notice

Practical exams for this module are conducted in off-grid terrain over a 48-hour period.
