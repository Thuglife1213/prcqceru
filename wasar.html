Water Search and Rescue

Course Code: ERU-WSR-01

Course Introduction

The Water Search and Rescue (WASAR) course is an elite training program designed for responders operating in aquatic environments. Given the Philippines' susceptibility to typhoons and flooding, this course focuses on swift-water rescue, boat handling, and victim extraction in hazardous water conditions.

Core Competencies

  • • Swift-water Swimming & Navigation
  • • Inflatable Rescue Boat (IRB) Ops
  • • Flood Incident Management
  • • Aquatic Medical Stabilization

Notice to Personnel

Physical fitness certification and basic swimming proficiency are mandatory prerequisites for this module.
