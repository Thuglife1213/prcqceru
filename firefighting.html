Basic Firefighting

Course Code: ERU-FRD-03

Course Introduction

Essential fire suppression training for ERU first responders. This module provides the knowledge required to utilize fire extinguishers, handle fire hoses, and support fire bureau operations while ensuring the safety of personnel and the public.

Core Competencies

  • • Chemistry of Fire & Behavior
  • • PPE and SCBA Familiarization
  • • Initial Suppression Techniques
  • • Search and Rescue in Smoke

Safety Notice

Personnel must undergo a respirator fit test prior to SCBA (Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus) modules.
