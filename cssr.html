Collapsed Structure Rescue

Course Code: ERU-CSS-02

Course Introduction

The CSSR course prepares ERU personnel for Urban Search and Rescue (USAR) scenarios. This technical module covers the location, extraction, and initial medical stabilization of victims trapped in collapsed buildings due to earthquakes or structural failures.

Core Competencies

  • • Structural Analysis & Shoring
  • • Concrete Breaching & Cutting
  • • Tech Search (Cameras/Listening)
  • • Confined Space Victim Access

Operational Protocol

This course adheres to INSARAG international standards for urban search and rescue operations.
